All About Dolphins

Dolphin Facts

Dolphins are fascinating creatures with unique characteristics. Here are some interesting facts:

  • Dolphins belong to the family Delphinidae.
  • They are known for their remarkable intelligence and problem-solving abilities.
  • Dolphins communicate with each other using a variety of clicks, whistles, and body movements.
  • There are various species of dolphins, including the Bottlenose Dolphin, Orca (Killer Whale), and Spinner Dolphin.

Dolphin Conservation

Conserving dolphin populations is crucial for maintaining the balance of marine ecosystems. Efforts are being made worldwide to protect dolphins from threats such as pollution, habitat loss, and fishing gear entanglement.
